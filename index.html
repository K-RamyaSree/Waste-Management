    Location

    Anantapuramu town is the Headquarters of the district. Its geographical co-ordinates are LAT 14º 68’ N and LONG 77º 60’ E. There are no zones/and 50 election wards in this Corporation.






    Eco-Friendly Practices

    Eco-friendly waste management is a way to dispose of waste without harming the environment. Some eco-friendly waste management methods include Recycling, Composting, Vermicomposting, Waste separation, Reusing.

    Environmental Issues

    Improper waste management can cause a number of environmental issues, including Air pollution, Water pollution, Soil contamination, Hazardous waste, Ecosystem damage, Climate change, Wildlife death, Mosquito breeding.

    Urban population

    Out of the population of 263898 population of the town, 74964 are living notified, non-notified slums and poor areas. Majority of them are leading their life as daily wage labourers, under employed labourers, seasonal labourers etc. At present there are 64poor settlements in the town, out of which 43are notified and 21non notified slums. Their participation in Government programmes like Janmma Bhoomi Programme, Clean & Green, Family Planning , Adult Education etc is immensely good, in fact they are the back bones to any kind of programme of the Government.

    Essential Safety Precautions in Waste Management

    Ensuring safety through proper waste handling, segregation, and hygiene practices.

    Use of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

    Always wear gloves, masks, goggles, and protective clothing to prevent exposure to hazardous materials.

    Health and Hygiene Practices

    Regularly wash hands, use hand sanitizers, and maintain personal hygiene to prevent the spread of contaminants.

    Safe Waste Storage and Disposal

    Properly store and dispose of waste in designated containers and facilities to ensure safe handling and environmental protection.

    Transportation Safety

    Ensure proper loading and securing of waste during transportation to prevent accidents and spills.

    Proper Waste Segregation

    Classify and separate waste into appropriate categories to facilitate recycling and safe disposal.

    Safe Handling Procedures

    Follow proper procedures to handle waste safely, including avoiding contact with hazardous materials and using appropriate equipment.
